\subsection{Detailed Description}
Header file for S\+DL 2D rendering functions.

This A\+PI supports the following features\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item single pixel points
\item single pixel lines
\item filled rectangles
\item texture images
\end{DoxyItemize}

The primitives may be drawn in opaque, blended, or additive modes.

The texture images may be drawn in opaque, blended, or additive modes. They can have an additional color tint or alpha modulation applied to them, and may also be stretched with linear interpolation.

This A\+PI is designed to accelerate simple 2D operations. You may want more functionality such as polygons and particle effects and in that case you should use S\+DL\textquotesingle{}s Open\+G\+L/\+Direct3D support or one of the many good 3D engines.

These functions must be called from the main thread. See this bug for details\+: \href{http://bugzilla.libsdl.org/show_bug.cgi?id=1995}{\tt http\+://bugzilla.\+libsdl.\+org/show\+\_\+bug.\+cgi?id=1995}
